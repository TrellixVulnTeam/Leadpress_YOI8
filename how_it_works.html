How it works

A few simple steps saving you time sourcing leads

Complete our callback form to arrange a chat

Tell us about your business; which job sectors you target and if you have any specific requirements. The more we know the better we can match leads to your needs.

Our lead generation experts get to work

We start building your list of leads with all the relevant information you've provided. We do this manually, strictly no bots or outsourcing.

Leads emailed to you every Sunday

We work throughout the week looking for suitable leads, once a week you'll get a beautiful Google sheet ready to go for Monday.

Everything starts with a chinwag!

Our partnership approach ensures a results first gameplan

Chat

Before we start searching the web for your ideal leads we first need to know exactly the type of leads you're interested in. This typically is an informal chat over the phone at a time that best suits you. This usually involves finding out about you and your agency, what details are most important to you and what you kind of leads are of no use to you.

Research

Now that we have all the relevant information, it's time for us to start compiling your list of leads, this is done over a 7 day period to ensure that you get a fair share of the latest jobs by HR Execs looking for you to fill their positions. We search high and low, from job boards to company websites and social media platforms.

Deliver

Every Sunday evening your leads will be sent to you via email - whilst we work on your customisable dashboard that is. You will receive the name of the person recruiting for the position, as well as their contact details including phone and emaill address. A full job spec will be provided with links to any LinkedIn accounts and to job adds posted online.
